Compact Fluorescent Lamps

Typically compact fluorescent lamps will pay for themselves in less than 9 months.

Compact Fluorescent lights will continue to save you money on your bill each month.

Light-Emitting Diodes

Most light emitting diodes only use 20-25% of energy.

Light emitting diodes last 15-25 times longer than other bulbs.

Phantom Energy

Unplug devices and appliances when they are not being used.

Phantom energy makes up to 23% of household's electricity usage.

Phantom energy costs the average family $165 per year.
